ABOUT MMC

MMC MISSION AND VISION:

  • Build capacities and competencies for the Muslim youths and prepare them for challenges of the future.
  • Develop self-sustaining Muslim entrepreneurs and business owners with capacity to support the future demands of the Ummah, in terms of employment, support for the poor and funding assistance for Islamic programs.
  • Build a new generation of visionary, well-trained, competitive and Godfearing Muslim leaders across various sectors of the Nigerian economy, including political governance.
  • Provoke Muslim youths to discover their purpose, provide them with necessary skills and ensure that they are leaders in their communities.

OUR ACTIVITIES:

In order to meet these objectives, MMC have outlined the following objectives on annual basis;

Direct mentoring scheme:
This is a six-monthly program that involves assigning direct mentors to Muslim professionals and entrepreneurs in early stages of career, including students within established time range and based on defined criteria.
Digital Presence:
This involves providing continued enlightenment, guidance, education and mentoring through regular sessions conducted on the online platforms.
Activation Foundry:
The seminar holds on a quarterly basis. This initiative provides an avenue for participants to learn critical employability skills and other career growth skills from senior Muslim experts. This shall hold across States of the federation, insha Allah.
Skill-up Training Session:
This involves holding regular core skill training sessions. This is a completely special training session dedicated to training participants on critical skills aimed at deepening their capacities and competencies.
Regular School Visits:
Making regular visits to higher institutions with a view to providing career guidance and hand-holding to Muslim students.
Mock Interviews or Pitch Session:
Providing mock interview sessions to members ahead of their scheduled job interview sessions, or mock pitch presentation for those defending business projects either for customerâ€™s buying, or for funding.
In addition to the above, we also have plans to set up incubation centers and organize Local Imam Development Programs in the nearest future.
