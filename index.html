Welcome to Muslim Mentorship Connect

Muslim Mentorship Connect (MMC) was set up to provoke Muslim youths to discover their purpose, provide them with necessary skills and ensure that they are leaders in their communities...

VISION

To build a new generation of visionary, well-trained, competitive and Godfearing Muslim leaders across various sectors of the Nigerian economy, including political governance.

MISSION

MMC was founded with a desire to develop self-sustaining Muslim entrepreneurs and business owners with a collective capacity to support the future demands of the Ummah, in terms of employment, support for the poor and funding assistance for the propagation of Islam
